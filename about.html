My Story

Hi, I am Shivam , A 17 year old app developer from India.

I have been creating android apps since I was 15 . I have started my coding journey as an android developer . It all started with a google search . I was just searching about android ROMs and suddenly got a suggestion in google that how to create an android app. After searching that , I never looked back . I just got fasinated by this android development stuff and just dreamt of becoming an android developer one day .But it is a professional course and it hasn't been taught in colleges in India So I just decided to learn online. After few research , I came to know that there is a software called android studio which is used to create an android app . I just set that studio up on my PC . I didn't even know how to install it , I just did next next and install and it was done although it took some time but it was worth it . After setting it up when I opened it I didn't even understand the single piece of that software. It was just too much for me. Then I did few more research and got to know that in order to create I need to learn java and XML . So I enrolled in few courses online for learning java and Android development in platforms like Acadgild , Udacity etc. Well the course was over , I learnt a lot there but still haven't worked on any real life app project . So I just decided to work for free and have created few apps for some Resellers. It really helped me a lot and boosted my confidence . After that I did freelancing and created some ecommerce android apps for the clients. The biggest problem for me was that I need to share the freelancing money with the server side developer. So I just decided to go full stack . I learned Node JS Express and Mongo DB as it is pretty easier to create REST APIs using these technologies . These days I am learning React and hopefully very soon I will become good at it .
